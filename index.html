01 / PROBLEM
Banks act too late
Traditional systems only trigger after a missed payment — when recovery costs are already high and customer trust is damaged. Prevenza AI identifies stress 2–4 weeks earlier.
02 / DETECTION
Behavioural signals are the key
Salary delay, savings decline, failed auto-debits — each event raises the risk score using transparent, auditable rules. No black boxes. Every decision is explainable.
03 / RESULT
Intervene at exactly the right moment
When the score crosses 70, the bank is alerted. Payment holidays, EMI extensions, or gentle reminders are deployed before the customer struggles — preserving the relationship.
